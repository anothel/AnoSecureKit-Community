# AnoSecureKit Product Boundary

AnoSecureKit Community is the public MPL-2.0 C++20 repository. OpenSSL 3.x is its
only shipped production provider.

AnoSecureKit Enterprise and AnoCrypto-C are separate repositories and products.
Community does not contain their proprietary source, adapters, policies,
packages, evidence, or validation status.

The canonical ownership and change-routing contract is
`docs/CROSS_REPOSITORY_BOUNDARY.md`.
